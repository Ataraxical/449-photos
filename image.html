Legplates of the Endless Void

Legplates of the endless void are generally considered the best off-set piece for tanks phase 2 (Secrets of Ulduar) of World of Warcraft: Wrath of the Lich King Classic. Their high item level (iLvl 252) gives them an unusually large stat budget and all of it goes to effective hitpoints (EHP) with high stamina and armor and avoidance with defense, dodge, and parry. Adding appropriate gems to the sockets to gain the socket bonus gives 8 defense rating or hit, 8 strength or expertise, and 60 stamina, giving this item a massive 238 stamina contributing greatly to a tank's EHP.
